Ideas I Live By

1. Don't doubt it. Once you start something with a clear visualized end result, then you are bound to face challenges along the way. It's almost suspicious if anything is without a challenge. The main point here is to not doubt it when you eventually face the challenges. A great boost would be to remind yourself why you started in the first place, it'll help to make sense of why you're investing your time and energy on it.

2. Look around, notice things. As human beings, we have a tendency to get lost in ourselves and take no notice of our surroundings. It may be either our friends and family, or a stranger, or anything around you really. It's neccessary to be present in the moment at all times. While you're worrying about the socks you wore that day, your friend might be going through some family crisis. I have a bad habit of being oblivious to the things around me. I'm trying to work on myself and trying to notice the little things in my surrounding.

3. Innovate for the better. There are countless problems our community is facing right now. It's great to have a hand in coming up with solutions for the people around you. Helping out even in a small way is not something to take lightly. Always try to make things better for other people who are in need of it.
